VIHAAN 8.0

PRESENTED BY IEEE DTU

VIHAAN is a 24-hour long hackathon that provides a platform for budding programmers to come up with solutions to real-world problems using technology. Students participate in teams of up to 4 members across various tracks. To add to the experience and learning quotient, teams that make it past the hackathon are awarded various cash prizes and goodies.
